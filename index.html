Scented Bliss

Exquisite Aroma - Exceptional

Fragrance Notes: Fresh bursts, Jasmine, Lily Flowers.

Dive into the luxurious allure of "Exquisite Aroma Exceptional You," a fragrance crafted to embody the essence of elegance, strength, and individuality. This scent doesn’t just sit on the skin; it melds with your very presence, creating a captivating aura that speaks volumes about sophistication and refinement. Opening with a burst of refreshing bergamot and zesty lemon, it invites you into a world of freshness and vitality. As the fragrance settles, delicate heart notes of jasmine and white lily add a graceful floral complexity, blending seamlessly with your natural scent to create a harmonious, enchanting experience. These middle notes evoke memories of blossoming gardens at dusk, symbolizing both beauty and mystery.

Legend Gold

Fragrance Notes: Amber, Musk, Rose, Oud, and Jasmine.

The fragrance opens with an invigorating blend of citrus and aromatic spices, setting a fresh, bold tone that captures attention. At its heart, delicate notes of rose and oud create a deeply resonant experience—one that’s warm, mysterious, and perfectly balanced. The rose lends a hint of sweetness, while the oud adds a distinctive richness that feels both powerful and graceful. As the fragrance settles, base notes of amber, musk, and sandalwood emerge, grounding the scent in earthy warmth and providing a lingering depth. This long-lasting finish makes "Legend Gold Deen & Noor" unforgettable, creating a trail that speaks of class and luxury.

1 Million

Fragrance Notes: Ment, Blood Orange, Lemon, Cinnamon and Cedarwood.

As the fragrance evolves, it reveals a warm and inviting base composed of luxurious notes of cedarwood, smooth leather, and rich amber. These elements work together to create a captivating and enduring scent that lingers on the skin, leaving a memorable trail. The balance between the fresh top notes and the warm base makes "One Million" versatile, suitable for both daytime wear and evening occasions. The packaging of "One Million" is as striking as the fragrance itself. Housed in a bold, gold ingot-shaped bottle, it exudes an air of extravagance and refinement. This design not only reflects the opulence of the scent but also serves as a statement piece on any dresser.

Coco Mademoiselle

Fragrance Notes: Bergamot, Orange, Lily of the Valley, Vanilla and Tonka Bean.

Coco Mademoiselle is often described as fresh, vibrant, and elegant, making it suitable for various occasions, from daywear to evening events. Its chic and sophisticated profile is emblematic of the modern woman who embodies confidence and charm. The iconic bottle design, featuring clean lines and a minimalist aesthetic, reflects the timeless elegance of Chanel and complements the luxurious experience of this exquisite fragrance. With its rich blend of notes, Coco Mademoiselle has become a staple in the world of perfumery, celebrated for its versatility and allure.
